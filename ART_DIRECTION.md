# Olympus Brawler Art Direction

## Goal

Olympus Brawler should look like a finished 2D platform fighter, not a web prototype. The target is painterly, mythic, high-contrast game art with original character designs, original UI, original animation language, and original arenas.

The style may share the broad category of modern mythic action art, but it must not copy another game's character shapes, costumes, portraits, UI motifs, animation poses, color keys, or story presentation.

## Production Standard

- Resolution: fighters should be authored at high resolution and exported around 220-320 px tall in gameplay scale.
- Silhouette: every fighter must be identifiable in black shape alone.
- Lighting: one strong key light, one colored rim light, and readable shadow masses.
- Texture: painterly brushwork and material separation, not flat fills.
- Readability: attacks must read at gameplay speed before detail is considered successful.
- Originality: no traced, copied, or style-matched production assets from other games.

## Fighter Asset Set

Each fighter needs these game-ready exports:

- `idle`: 8-12 frames
- `run`: 10-14 frames
- `jump`: 4-6 frames
- `fall`: 4-6 frames
- `hurt`: 3-5 frames
- `ko`: 6-10 frames
- `quick`: 6-10 frames
- `heavy`: 8-12 frames
- `special`: 10-16 frames
- `ult`: 16-28 frames

Each fighter also needs these UI/story assets:

- Full-body character sheet
- Gameplay sprite sheet or atlas
- Select icon
- Large portrait
- Story dialogue bust
- Ability/VFX color key

## First Production Characters

### Zeus

- Visual fantasy: storm king, regal authority, explosive divine voltage.
- Shape language: broad shoulders, vertical lightning motifs, strong cape or sash movement.
- Palette: ivory, storm gold, deep navy, electric yellow-white.
- Gameplay read: medium-large silhouette, projectile posture should be unmistakable.
- Signature effects: forked lightning, charged air, circular thunder shock on ultimate.

### Athena

- Visual fantasy: disciplined war wisdom, shield mastery, tactical precision.
- Shape language: spear angles, shield circle, owl/olive hints used subtly.
- Palette: pale steel, marble white, muted blue, restrained gold.
- Gameplay read: faster and cleaner than Zeus; defensive poses should look braced and intelligent.
- Signature effects: shield arcs, spear glints, geometric counter flashes.

## Roster Visual Hooks

- Ares: brutal red-metal war pressure, jagged armor, heavy impact sparks.
- Poseidon: sea glass, bronze, wet cloth motion, cresting wave VFX.
- Artemis: moonlit greens, silver bow shapes, fast cloak/hood movement.
- Hades: violet-black shadow, ember undertones, smoke and chains as motion accents.
- Heracles: heavy heroic mass, lion pelt cues, blunt-force dust and stone.
- Achilles: bright gold duelist, spear speed lines, confident narrow stance.
- Odysseus: travel-worn cleverness, muted palette, trick decoys and angled motion.

## Stage Production Set

Each arena needs:

- Background plate
- Midground parallax layer
- Foreground platform art
- Foreground occlusion/decor layer
- Lighting overlay
- Hazard or interactive concept
- Mood palette
- Thumbnail/select image

### Mount Olympus

- Mood: sunlit divine summit with dramatic cloud depth.
- Palette: marble white, gold, sky blue, storm shadow.
- Platform idea: carved marble platforms with gold inlay and cloud shadow.
- Hazard idea: delayed lightning marks a small section before striking.

### Poseidon's Temple

- Mood: submerged sacred ruin with refracted light.
- Palette: teal, seafoam, bronze, dark blue-green.
- Platform idea: wet stone and coral-bronze fixtures.
- Hazard idea: wave surge pushes fighters horizontally.

### Underworld

- Mood: royal darkness, violet fire, heavy air.
- Palette: black-violet, bone, ember, cold blue.
- Platform idea: obsidian shelves with ghostly rim light.
- Hazard idea: shadow hands briefly slow or lift fighters.

## VFX Standards

- Every hit needs a directional slash/spark, small particle burst, and brief camera response.
- Heavy hits need a larger flash, stronger shake, and short hit pause once implemented.
- Projectiles need a head, trail, impact, and dissipation.
- Ultimates need a charge tell, screen flash, large silhouette moment, and aftermath particles.
- Shields need a visible bubble, shield damage feedback, and break stun effect.

## UI Standards

- HUD should be compact, readable, and stable at 1280x720.
- Fighter portraits should replace plain text once production portraits exist.
- Damage should be visually dominant; lives, shield, and ultimate meter are secondary.
- Menus should use mythic materials without becoming ornate enough to slow scanning.

## Production Priority

1. Zeus gameplay sprite sheet plus portrait.
2. Athena gameplay sprite sheet plus portrait.
3. Mount Olympus background and platform art.
4. Lightning, shield, spear, and generic hit VFX.
5. Poseidon and Hades story-opponent sprites.
6. Remaining roster and stage art.
